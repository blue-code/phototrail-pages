# PhotoTrail Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2026-04-14**

Blue Code ("we", "us", "our") operates the PhotoTrail mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Photo Library Access
The App accesses your device's photo library to read EXIF metadata (camera settings, GPS coordinates, timestamps) and display photo thumbnails. Photos are processed **entirely on your device** and are never uploaded to any server.

### 1.2 Location Data
The App uses location data in two ways:
- **Photo GPS Data**: Reading and correcting GPS coordinates embedded in your photos' EXIF metadata.
- **Apple Watch GPS Tracking** (optional): Recording your location trail while shooting, stored locally on your device.

### 1.3 Reverse Geocoding
When displaying location names for photo coordinates, the App uses Apple's MapKit geocoding service. Only coordinates are sent to Apple's servers to retrieve place names. This is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external server.
- We do **not** use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.
- We do **not** require account creation or login.
- We do **not** access your contacts, microphone, or any data beyond photos and location.

## 3. Data Storage

All data (trip information, photo metadata, corrected GPS coordinates) is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. No cloud synchronization is performed.

## 4. Data Sharing

When you use the "Share" or "PDF Export" feature, the App generates an image or PDF document on your device. Sharing is performed through the iOS system share sheet — we do not intercept or store shared content.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following Apple system services:
- **MapKit** — for map display and reverse geocoding
- **Photos Framework** — for accessing your photo library
- **WatchConnectivity** — for Apple Watch communication

No third-party SDKs or services are used.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App does not require any personal information to function.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Deleting a trip within the App removes all associated metadata.
- Uninstalling the App removes all stored data completely.
- Original photos in your photo library are never modified or deleted by the App.

## 8. Your Rights

You can:
- Revoke photo library or location permissions at any time via iOS Settings.
- Delete all App data by uninstalling the App.
- Export your trip data as PDF before deletion.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
